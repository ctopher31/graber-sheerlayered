Top Treatments

Wood Cornices

Available in several styles and heights, cornices conceal headrails and hardware to add a bold design element to the top of a window.

Wood Valances

Offered in a variety of styles, coordinating paints, and stains, valances create a dramatic, clean line whether your shade is mounted inside or outside the window frame.

Keystones

These accents cover splices in valances and cornices, or simply add visual interest. Keystones can be painted or stained in matching or contrasting colors.

Fabric-Wrapped Cornices

Complement or accent your shades with a fabric-wrapped cornice in your choice of fabric. Opt to upgrade to a premium fabric-wrapped cornice for a more elegant touch.

Soft Top Treatments

Add depth to your design with soft fabric detail. Available in a variety of top treatment styles
